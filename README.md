# Crescent State: Wax & Wane

Place your orb each day. Watch your constellation grow.

Find peace in your Crescent State.


**How it works**

Drag the orb to where you feel on the grid. Calm to activated left to right, unpleasant to pleasant bottom to top. That's it. Come back tomorrow.

**The moon**

The moon above the grid shows today's real lunar phase. Your mood history appears as a constellation of colored moons in *your moons*, each lit by the phase on that day.

**Your data**

Everything lives in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere. Use export / import to back up or move your data.

I hope you enjoy and find peace in your crescent state.
